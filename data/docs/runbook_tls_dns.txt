Title: Runbook - Network, DNS, and TLS Troubleshooting
Type: runbook
Tags: dns, tls, handshake, certificates, network

DNS Troubleshooting Procedure:
Step 1: Verify hostname resolution.
nslookup api.internal.company.com
dig api.internal.company.com

Step 2: Measure DNS lookup latency across regions.
Step 3: Check DNS record TTL values for expected propagation behavior.

TLS Troubleshooting:
TLS handshake failures may appear as intermittent connection resets or SSL errors.

Diagnostic Commands:
openssl s_client -connect api.internal.company.com:443
curl -v https://api.internal.company.com

Things to verify:
- certificate validity and expiration
- certificate authority chain correctness
- TLS protocol version compatibility

Operational Best Practices:
- automate certificate renewal
- enable TLS monitoring alerts
- rotate certificates before expiration
